No Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing ForgeIndia Connect for your career guidance and job consulting needs. Please carefully read our No Refund Policy regarding the enrollment fee before proceeding with your payment.

1. Non-Refundable Enrollment Fee

The enrollment fee of ₹1500 (Fifteen Hundred Indian Rupees) is charged for our specialized "Dedicated Guidance Support" service. This fee is strictly non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances.

2. Purpose of the Fee

This fee covers the administrative and operational costs involved in:

  • Profiling and assessment of your resume.
  • Dedicated support from our career counselors.
  • Processing your application through our job portal networks.
  • Initial consultation and guidance services.

3. Acknowledgment

By clicking the "I agree to the No Refund Policy" checkbox on the enrollment form and proceeding with the payment, you explicitly acknowledge that you understand and accept that this fee is not refundable, regardless of the outcome of the job application process or if you choose to discontinue our services.

Questions? If you have any queries regarding this policy, please contact us at info@forgeindiaconnect.com before making the payment.
